About Me

Hello! I'm Nishita Singh, a passionate and driven cybersecurity enthusiast and full-stack web developer. I’m currently pursuing my degree in Computer Science with a specialization in Cybersecurity and Penetration Testing from the prestigious National Forensic Sciences University, and also enrolled in the BS in Data Science program at IIT Madras.

My interest in programming sparked back in high school and has evolved into a strong foundation in areas such as web development, AI/ML, deep learning, and digital forensics.

I’ve earned top positions in global and national hackathons — including 1st in HackX 2.0 (NIET Noida), 3rd in ForkIT Hackathon (IIIT Delhi), and ranked 9th in the Toyota Tsusho Systems CTF (Worldwide).

My goal is to keep blending creativity and logic to develop impactful, secure, and efficient software solutions that solve real-world problems. Let's connect and collaborate!
